# Fantasy Hero Quest

An AI-powered narrative RPG mobile app where you create your hero and embark on text-based adventures driven by an AI Game Master. Inspired by Stranger Things and classic tabletop RPGs.

## Features

### Onboarding & World Customization
- **5-Step Onboarding Flow**: Guided setup when you first launch the app
- **Language Selection**: Choose your preferred language at the start (11 languages) - **AI responds in your chosen language!**
- **Genre Selection**: Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Horror, Mystery, Adventure, Post-Apocalyptic, Superhero, Mythology
- **Era Selection**: Pick from 150+ unique time periods and settings with **category filters** for easier browsing
- **Tone & Difficulty**: 40 unique story tones organized by category with 4 difficulty levels
- **AI Personality**: Configure how the AI Game Master narrates (Dramatic, Mysterious, Friendly, Dark, Epic)
- **Genre-Era Compatibility**: Smart recommendations for eras that match your chosen genre
- **Filter System**: Browse eras by category (Original, Fantasy, Sci-Fi, Horror, Historical, Modern, Adventure, Mythology, Pop Culture, and more) - reduces loading and makes selection easier

### Fully Localized AI Game Master
The AI Game Master speaks your language! All story narration, choices, and responses are generated in your selected language:
- English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Japanese, Korean, Chinese, Russian, Arabic
- Dynamic story generation adapts to your era, genre, and tone settings
- Conversation history maintains context for coherent storytelling

### Dice Rolling System
- Full D&D-style dice (d4, d6, d8, d10, d12, d20)
- Animated dice rolls with haptic feedback
- Attribute modifiers applied automatically
- Critical success/failure detection
- **Game Master-triggered dice checks** - dice only appear when the AI requests a skill check
- **Dynamic DC (Difficulty Class)** - AI sets appropriate difficulty based on the action
- Success/failure outcomes affect story progression

### Encounter System
- **Dynamic encounters** based on location and level
- Multiple encounter types: Exploration, Combat, Social, Puzzle, Discovery, Trap, Rest, Merchant, Boss
- **Choice-based outcomes** with dice roll checks
- Attribute and skill requirements for certain choices
- Rewards: XP, items, gold, relationship changes, quest progress
- **Stranger Things encounters**: Flickering lights, school basement mysteries, Demodog fights, meeting allies

### Combat System
- Enemy encounters with stats (health, attack, defense)
- Enemy abilities and special attacks
- Weakness/resistance system tied to attributes
- Loot tables for defeated enemies
- Boss encounters with unique mechanics

### Quest System
- Main story quests with multiple objectives
- Side quests and personal missions
- Daily quests with timed rewards
- Progress tracking with completion rewards
- Quest status: Active, Completed, Failed

### Skills System
- **Combat Skills**: Melee, Ranged, Defense
- **Social Skills**: Persuasion, Intimidation, Deception
- **Survival Skills**: Stealth, Athletics, Perception
- **Knowledge Skills**: Investigation, Lore, Technology
- **Mystical Skills**: Sixth Sense, Willpower (unlock via prerequisites)
- Train skills to gain XP and level up

### World Map
- Interactive map with discovered locations
- Location danger levels (1-5)
- Connected pathways between areas
- Discover new locations through exploration
- Stranger Things era: Downtown Hawkins, School, Byers House, Mirkwood Forest, Hawkins Lab, The Upside Down

### Journal / Codex
- Story entries recorded automatically
- Discovery logs for items and locations
- Character notes and lore entries
- Filter by entry type
- Era-tagged content

### Inventory System
- Item collection during adventures
- Item types: Weapons, Armor, Consumables, Keys, Artifacts
- Rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, Legendary
- Usable consumables for health/energy

### NPC Relationships
- Meet characters throughout your adventure
- Relationship system (-100 to +100)
- Dynamic relationship states: Enemy to Devoted
- Character discovery as you explore
- Era-specific NPCs (Sheriff Hopper, Eleven, Steve, etc.)

### Daily Challenges
- Three daily challenges that reset each day
- Challenge types: Dice, Explore, Social, Combat, Story
- XP and Gold rewards for completion
- Progress tracking throughout the day

### Player Statistics
- Total play time tracking
- Sessions played counter
- Dice statistics (rolls, crits, failures)
- Quest completion stats
- NPCs met and locations discovered
- Achievement progress

### Achievements

### Settings
